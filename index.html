A Tribute to "The Iron Man of India"


Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel

About Him

Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel was one of the greatest leaders of India's Freedom struggle. He played a major role in integrating more than 560 Princely States into the Indian Union. Due to his strong Determination and Leadership, he is known as the "Iron Man of India".

Major Achievements

  • Integrated 562 Princely States into independent India.
  • First Deputy Prime Minister and Home Minister of India.
  • Leader of Bardoli Satyagraha.
  • Organised modern All India Services (IAS,IPS etc).
  • Played crucial role in Indian Freedom movement.

Contribution to the Nation

Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel ensured the political unity of India after independence. His Administrative vision laid the foundation of India's strong governance. Without his efforts, India might have remained divided into multiple kingdoms.
